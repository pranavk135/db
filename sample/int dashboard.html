Who We Are

We are a group of driven and curious final-year Computer Science diploma students from The Oxford Polytechnic. With a shared vision to explore innovation in cybersecurity, we took on a project to make a real-world impact:

“Network Traffic Visualization Coupled with Convolutional Neural Networks for Enhanced IoT Botnet Detection.”

Our Mission

With the exponential growth of IoT devices comes a growing threat—botnets. Our mission was to enhance IoT security using intelligent, real-time solutions. We combined data visualization with Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to detect malicious traffic before it becomes a breach.

What We Built

- A custom interactive dashboard to visualize IoT traffic patterns
- A trained CNN model capable of identifying botnet activity
- A modular and scalable architecture for real-world deployment
- A unified vision of practical innovation in cybersecurity

Our Stack

Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
Visualization: Chart.js, D3.js
Backend: Python, Flask
Machine Learning: TensorFlow, Keras
Tools: Wireshark, VS Code, GitHub

Where We’re Headed

We're aiming to expand our model’s accuracy with real-world datasets, bring our system to live IoT environments, and scale it into a cloud-based security platform. The journey doesn't end here—it’s just the beginning.
