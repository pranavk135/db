Network Traffic Visualization Coupled with Convolutional Neural Networks for Enhanced IoT Botnet Detection

The Internet of Things (IoT) is revolutionizing industries, but its widespread adoption has also introduced significant security risks. IoT botnets—malicious networks of compromised devices—are increasingly used in cyberattacks. Our project addresses this challenge by combining network traffic visualization with Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to detect and mitigate botnet threats in real-time, providing enhanced protection for IoT ecosystems.
