Introduction

The exponential growth of Internet of Things (IoT) devices has introduced new challenges in network security. Traditional Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS) often struggle to efficiently detect sophisticated botnet attacks targeting IoT networks. To address this, our project integrates network traffic visualization with Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to enhance detection capabilities.

By converting raw network traffic data into visual representations, we enable CNNs to analyze patterns and anomalies effectively. This approach leverages the strength of CNNs in image classification to identify malicious activities within network traffic, providing a robust solution for IoT botnet detection.
